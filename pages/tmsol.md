# The Legend of Lemur: The Many Sides of Lemur
---
![Prophecy of War Cover](https://img.wattpad.com/cover/157897625-256-k249826.jpg)

## Description:
You may have heard of Lemur's story, but this world is large and hides many secrets in it's most mysterious corners. This book tells the life stories of Gladius, Jackal, and Calvin. Gladius, Lemur's evil twin brother that was separated at birth. Jackal, Lemur's alter from a dimension where feeding on human flesh is a good thing. Finally, Calvin, an unfortunate alter of Lemur's who has gone through so much in his life, that he wants to end it. Live in the shoes of these three people and learn their life battles in this amazing book!
